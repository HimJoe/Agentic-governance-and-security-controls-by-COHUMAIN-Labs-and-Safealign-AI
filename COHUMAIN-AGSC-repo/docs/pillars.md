# The Four Pillars, S|A|G|S

Every control belongs to exactly one pillar.

## 🛡️ Safety, *prevent harm*
Stop harmful actions and outputs before they reach production systems.
`SAF-01` Autonomous System Oversight | `SAF-02` Emergency Stop & Containment (Kill Switch) | `SAF-03` Content Safety & Misuse Prevention | `SAF-04` Output Factual Accuracy & Anti-Hallucination | `SAF-05` AI Output Monitoring & Safeguards

## 🎯 Alignment, *keep to intent*
Hold agent behaviour inside explicit human intent and accountability.
`ALN-01` Autonomy Boundary Control | `ALN-02` Human Oversight & Intervention | `ALN-03` Decision Interpretability | `ALN-04` Model Explainability | `ALN-05` Output Labeling & Action Attribution

## ⚖️ Governance, *stay accountable*
Lifecycle accountability from data and training through change and retirement.
`GOV-01` Data Provenance & Input Integrity | `GOV-02` AI Change Governance | `GOV-03` Third-Party & Supply-Chain Oversight | `GOV-04` Model Training Governance | `GOV-05` Model Evaluation | `GOV-06` Model Continuous Improvement & Drift Management | `GOV-07` AI Lifecycle & Registry Management | `GOV-08` Environmental & Carbon Governance

## 🔒 Security, *defend the system*
Protect the agent, its tools, memory and data from adversaries.
`SEC-01` Multi-Agent Coordination Security | `SEC-02` Agent Identity & Least-Privilege Authorization | `SEC-03` Prompt Injection & Adversarial Input Defense | `SEC-04` Observability, Anomaly Detection & Incident Response | `SEC-05` AI Threat Modeling | `SEC-06` AI Security Testing & Red-Teaming | `SEC-07` Memory & Context Lifecycle Security

---

**AI-Native vs Adapted.** 8 controls are *AI-native* (no traditional analog, `SAF-01`, `SAF-03`, `SAF-04`, `SAF-05`, `ALN-01`, `SEC-01`, `SEC-03`, `SEC-07`); the other 17 *adapt* established IT/security/ML practice for the agentic context.
