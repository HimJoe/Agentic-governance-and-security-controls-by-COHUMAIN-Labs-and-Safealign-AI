# Governance

## Stewardship
This standard is jointly stewarded by **COHUMAIN Labs** (human-AI governance, alignment and research) and **SafeAlign AI** (security, observability and runtime enforcement). No single party commercially gates the specification.

## Decision-making
- **Editorial changes** (typos, clarifications, link fixes): any maintainer may merge.
- **Mapping changes** (MIT/ATLAS/ATT&CK/regulatory): require review by one maintainer from each steward.
- **Control changes** (add/remove/re-scope a control, change a pillar or tier): require an RFC issue, a comment period of at least 14 days, and consensus of the maintainers.

## Versioning
Semantic versioning. Control-set changes increment the minor or major version; the `CHANGELOG.md` records every release. Each release tags the workbook and the JSON/CSV together so they never drift.

## Neutrality
The standard is vendor-neutral. Mappings to commercial tooling (including the stewards' own products) are illustrative only and never required for conformance.
